## Project Description

### 🎯 Problem
Legal contracts are complex and filled with technical jargon. Most people don't understand what they're signing, leading to unfavorable clauses, hidden risks, and potential legal troubles. Non-lawyers need an easy way to analyze contracts, identify red flags, and understand their implications **before** signing.

**Key Issues:**
- Contracts use confusing legal language
- Users miss critical clauses (non-compete, IP assignment, arbitration, etc.)
- No guidance on negotiation strategies
- No comparison with industry standards
- Takes days/weeks to get lawyer reviews (expensive)

### ✅ Solution
**LegalEase AI** uses cutting-edge **GPT-4 AI** to instantly analyze legal documents and provide:

1. **Risk Identification** - AI spots problematic clauses with severity ratings
2. **Risk Scoring** (0-100) - Clear numerical assessment
3. **Decision Recommendation** - "Safe to Sign" / "Be Careful" / "Do Not Sign"
4. **Suggested Amendments** - AI proposes better clause wording
5. **Negotiation Tips** - Industry-standard recommendations
6. **Multi-Language Support** - English, Hindi, Hinglish (regional market)

**How It Works:**
```
User uploads/pastes contract 
    ↓
AI analyzes with GPT-4
    ↓
Instant risk assessment
    ↓
Actionable recommendations
    ↓
Export as PDF report
```
